Who are IPAA's Members?

Private and corporate membership is extensive and is drawn from Commonwealth and State
Government Agencies, Local Government, Government Business Enterprises, Statutory Authorities,
Public Bodies and the University. IPAA also has several private sector members, as our
organisation represents a key resource for gaining insight into current public sector management issues.
